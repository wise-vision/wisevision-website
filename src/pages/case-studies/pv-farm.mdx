      </div>
      <div className="card">
        <p className="body-lg">Manual inspections were time-consuming and difficult to standardize</p>
      </div>
      <div className="card">
        <p className="body-lg">Sensor data, drone imagery and robot telemetry were handled in separate systems</p>
      </div>

      </div>

<Section>
  <div className="container">
    <h2 className="display-md text-center mb-2xl">Solution</h2>
    
    <div className="grid grid--3">
      <div className="card">
        <h3 className="headline-md mb-md">1. WiseOS – Operational Layer</h3>
        <p className="body mb-md">WiseOS was deployed as the <strong>central operating layer</strong>:</p>
        <ul className="body">
          <li>LoRaWAN sensors integrated via the WiseOS <strong>LoRaWAN↔ROS 2 bridge</strong></li>
          <li>Drones and ground robots connected over ROS 2 with <strong>Zenoh-based transport</strong></li>
          <li>Telemetry stored in the <strong>Data Black Box (InfluxDB)</strong></li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      
      <div className="card">
        <h3 className="headline-md mb-md">2. MCP ROS2 – Mission Orchestration</h3>
        <p className="body mb-md">MCP ROS2 provided <strong>tool-based access</strong>:</p>
        <ul className="body">
          <li>List robots, sensors and available missions</li>
          <li>Pre-defined inspection workflows as MCP tools</li>
          <li>Launch drones, subscribe to feeds, log events</li>
          <li>Script missions without low-level glue code</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      
      <div className="card">
        <h3 className="headline-md mb-md">3. Digital Twin – Validation</h3>
        <p className="body mb-md">A <strong>Digital Twin</strong> using O3DE / Isaac Sim / Genesis:</p>
        <ul className="body">
          <li>3D layout of panel rows and substations</li>
          <li>Simulated robots and drones</li>
          <li>Integration with WiseOS and MCP ROS2</li>
          <li>End-to-end testing before live deployment</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Section>

<Section background="gradient">
  <div className="container">
    <h2 className="display-md mb-lg">Architecture Overview</h2>
    
    <div className="card" style={{background: 'var(--bg-primary)', padding: 'var(--space-xl)'}}>
      <ol className="body-lg" style={{lineHeight: '2'}}>
        <li><strong>LoRaWAN sensors</strong> → ChirpStack → WiseOS LoRaWAN bridge → ROS 2 topics</li>
        <li><strong>Robots & drones</strong> → ROS 2 nodes → WiseOS (Zenoh transport, Data Black Box)</li>
        <li><strong>MCP ROS2 server</strong> → exposes ROS 2 interfaces as MCP tools for operators/agents</li>
        <li><strong>Digital Twin</strong> → subscribes to ROS 2 topics and interacts with MCP ROS2 for simulated missions</li>
      </ol>
    </div>
  </div>
</Section>
